Speek is a chat platform for polyglots who want to practice a language.

This is our final project for Holberton School - Foundations in Computer Science. Through this platform, we have worked on the development of various skills that we have acquired over the year. This includes project planning, organization, development, testing, and project production.

Our platform's main objective is to assist polyglots in practicing and improving their skills in different languages. Whether you want to regain proficiency in a language or simply refine it, our project is designed to support you on this journey.

Send Messages in Real Time.

Send and receive messages instantly with users from around the world.
Practice with Friends.

Create unique and special conversations with your friends, learn alongside them, and reinforce your knowledge with those closest to you.
Practice with Strangers.

Interact with people from around the world in a unique way, expand your vocabulary, and enrich your experience with the cultural diversity that each user can offer you.

About us

What inspired us to create this?

As polyglots, we have experienced the loss of skills in certain languages over time. After years of dedication, we noticed that these skills would fade within months if we stopped practicing. We understood that neglecting to practice our knowledge and abilities leads to forgetfulness. It was then that we realized there was no practical and effective solution to revitalize our languages. Out of this need, the idea of Speek was born: to provide a platform that allows polyglots to practice any language easily and effectively. Our goal is to help you maintain and improve your language skills in a practical and efficient manner.
